The FleakOS system

FleakOS is a unix-like system, created with the idea of ​​having a current desktop, but with more security, speed and even greater power over you and the machine. Our philosophy revolves around user freedom and user experience, so less is more. You can contact us directly by Telegram, IRC or via Email.
